I recently bought a Harmony Remote control 525. I was pleasantly suprised to see that it had a MacOSX version! Most of it worked. The only thing that got me bugging was that I could not rename my activities. It would just have a text field but did not allow me to type anything in it.

I found the solution burried in :
http://forums.logitech.com/logitech/board/message?board.id=remote_software&message.id=3702


You need to close your browser when doing this. I guess the program actually used the Safari Webkit to work. And when another program uses the same Webkit, it will not focus correctly.

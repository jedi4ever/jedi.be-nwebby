--- title: "2009: The year of the Agile Sysadmin ?" author: Patrick Debois guid: https://www.jedi.be/blog/?p=600 created_at: 2009-03-05 10:21:18.0 +00:00 blog_post: true categories: - Agile: Development, Testing and Infrastructure tags: - sysadmin - infrastructure - agile2009 - agile --- On 3/03/2009 the submission deadline for Agile 2009 in Chicago has expired. They have hundreds of submissions. It's one of the largest conferences in the world around agile.
It's really nice to see 12 submissions related to bringing the field of sysadmins, infrastructure, service management closer to the Agile development, project management world. What excites me even more is that it is considered frontier stuff, an emerging field of new ideas.
You can help these submissions in getting selected by reviewing and commenting the proposals. (you do need to create a login for this) . Thanks for your help!

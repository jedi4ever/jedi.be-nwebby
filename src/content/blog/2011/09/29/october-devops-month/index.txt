>  So you are looking for a devops place to be in October? Be quick to register to one or both of these conferences

## Europe: Devopsdays Goteborg

On *Oct 14 - 15 in Goteborg*, we're organizing the third! [Devopsdays Europe (goteborg)](http://devopsdays.org/events/2011-goteborg/)

<img src="http://devopsdays.org/images/devopsdays-banner-small.png">

Here's a list of the talks:

- Ramon Van Alteren , [Hiring and embedding new team members at Hyves](http://www.devopsdays.org/events/2011-goteborg/proposals/Hiring%20and%20embedding%20new%20team%20members%20at%20Hyves/)
- Mitchell Hashimoto , [DevOps is not an absolute. It's a range](http://www.devopsdays.org/events/2011-goteborg/proposals/DevOps%20is%20not%20an%20absolute%20-%20It%27s%20a%20range/)
- Mattias Jansson and Noa Resare , [A case study in operations and development integration at Spotify](http://www.devopsdays.org/events/2011-goteborg/proposals/A%20case%20study%20in%20ops%20and%20dev%20integration%20at%20Spotify/)
- Julian Simpson , [Everything I learned about Continuous Integration , I learned from System Administration](http://www.devopsdays.org/events/2011-goteborg/proposals/Everything%20I%20learned%20from%20CI%20-%20I%20learned%20from%20a%20sysadmin/)
- John Clapham and Paul Swartout , [Dev+Ops+Org - How we are including (almost) everyone](http://www.devopsdays.org/events/2011-goteborg/proposals/dev-ops-org-how%20we%20are%20including%20everyone/)
- John Vincent , [Sometimes it *IS* about the tools - Solving cultural issues with software](http://www.devopsdays.org/events/2011-goteborg/proposals/sometimes%20it%20is%20about%20the%20tools/)

For more information on the schedule , goto [the complete program](http://devopsdays.org/events/2011-goteborg/program/)
